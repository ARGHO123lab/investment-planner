Why Use This SWP Calculator?

A Systematic Withdrawal Plan (SWP) allows you to withdraw a fixed amount from your investments every month while the remaining balance continues to earn returns. This calculator helps estimate how long your retirement corpus may last under different withdrawal and market return assumptions.
