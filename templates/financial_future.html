How Does This Planner Work?

The planner estimates the monthly investment required based on your target amount, investment period, and expected investment returns. It also considers inflation to help you understand the actual purchasing power your future wealth may need. The calculations are designed for educational purposes and should be used as a planning guide rather than a guaranteed investment projection.

Common Financial Planning Mistakes

  • ❌ Starting investments too late.
  • ❌ Ignoring inflation.
  • ❌ Not reviewing financial goals annually.
  • ❌ Investing without diversification.
  • ❌ Stopping investments during market corrections.

📅 Annual SIP Increase Impact

If you increase your monthly investment by just 10% every year, you could potentially achieve your financial goal earlier or build a significantly larger corpus over the same period. This strategy, often called a Step-Up SIP, can help your investments keep pace with salary growth and inflation.
