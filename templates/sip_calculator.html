What is a SIP?

A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) is a disciplined way of investing in mutual funds where you invest a fixed amount at regular intervals, usually every month. Instead of investing a large amount at one time, SIP allows you to build wealth gradually. It also helps reduce the impact of market volatility through rupee cost averaging while allowing your investments to benefit from long-term compounding. SIPs are suitable for beginners, salaried professionals, students, and anyone looking to achieve long-term financial goals.

Why Invest Through SIP?

📈 Power of Compounding

Generate returns on both your investment and previous returns over time.

💰 Affordable

Start investing with small monthly amounts according to your budget.

📅 Disciplined Investing

Automatic monthly investing helps create a healthy financial habit.

📉 Rupee Cost Averaging

Buy more units when markets are low and fewer units when prices are high.
