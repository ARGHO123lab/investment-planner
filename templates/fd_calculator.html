💰 What is a Fixed Deposit?

A Fixed Deposit (FD) is a savings instrument offered by banks and financial institutions where you invest a lump sum for a fixed period at a predetermined interest rate. At maturity, you receive your original investment along with the accumulated interest.

Fixed Deposits are commonly used by investors seeking relatively stable returns and capital preservation.

📖 Understanding Your FD Results

Your maturity amount is made up of two components:

  • Your original deposit amount.
  • The interest earned during the investment period.

Generally, higher interest rates and longer investment durations result in greater maturity values due to the effect of compounding.

✅ Benefits of Fixed Deposits

  • Predictable returns.
  • Capital preservation.
  • Suitable for conservative investors.
  • Flexible investment tenures.
  • Simple and easy to understand.

⚠ Things to Consider Before Investing

  • Compare interest rates offered by different banks.
  • Check premature withdrawal rules.
  • Understand taxation on interest income.
  • Consider inflation while planning long-term investments.
  • Review your liquidity requirements before locking your funds.
