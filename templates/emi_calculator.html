💳 What is an EMI?

An Equated Monthly Installment (EMI) is the fixed amount you pay every month to repay a loan. Each EMI consists of two components:

  • Principal repayment
  • Interest charged by the lender

This calculator helps you estimate your monthly repayment so you can plan your finances before applying for a loan.

📖 Understanding Your EMI Result

Your monthly EMI depends on three factors:

  • Loan Amount
  • Interest Rate
  • Loan Tenure

Generally, longer loan tenures reduce monthly EMIs but increase the total interest paid over the life of the loan.
